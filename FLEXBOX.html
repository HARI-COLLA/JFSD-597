Fruits & Vegetables

Whole Grains

Whole grains provide essential fiber, vitamins, and minerals that promote heart health and maintain steady energy levels.

Healthy Picks: Oats, brown rice, quinoa, and whole wheat bread.

Lean Proteins

Protein is the building block of muscles and tissues. Lean protein sources help repair cells, boost metabolism, and support weight management.

Best Sources: Chicken, fish, eggs, tofu, and lentils.

Healthy Fats

Not all fats are bad! Healthy fats support brain function and heart health while keeping you full longer.

Best Choices: Avocados, nuts, olive oil, and fatty fish.

Hydration & Beverages

Water is essential for life. Staying hydrated keeps your organs functioning well, improves skin health, and prevents fatigue.

Good Choices: Water, herbal teas, coconut water, and smoothies.
